Do you love?

In this 20 minute film adaptation of Stephen King's short story of the same name, a destitute hitchhiking college dropout meets the girl of his fantasies at a local diner, stirring up fury-fueled bloodlust that she seems to encourage, almost too completely.

NONA, written and directed by Riley Card and Shaun Rousso, tackles themes of ego, masculinity, and emotional entitlement through the lens of a disallusioned drifter seeking attachment. In a midwestern setting, Stephen King's cold-blooded murders are reinterpreted with a modern spin, cool cinematic tone, and captivating performance of lust–-and bloodlust.



Meet The Team

Riley Card

Writer/Director


Riley Card is a student of Film & Media Studies and Linguistics. She has worked in production with Instigate Productions, management with Epicenter Agency, reality TV post production and music with ITV America, and even script data analytics with StoryFit. Riley loves to write and is the author of an episodic sci fi novel called The Future Chronicles. In her spare time, you can find Riley running in forest park, exploring the city, or hiking with her friends and two little sisters.

Shaun Rousso

Writer/Director


Shaun Rousso is a senior studying Film Production and Computer Science. He has been a storyteller since the second grade — when he developed a short story series about a super-powered chicken parmesan. Most recently, Shaun wrote and directed a full-length psychological horror play. As an avid film lover, he is ecstatic to bridge his creative sensibilities with the work of one of the greatest artists in the horror genre.

Barrett Wagner

Producer


Barrett Wagner is a current fourth-year BSBA Marketing, Film and Media Studies student at Washington University in St. Louis. She has a background in theatre production and management as well as film production assistance in the St Louis area. Also an avid reader and horror fan, Barrett is so excited to bring Stephen King’s story to life and hopes you are just as excited to watch it!
